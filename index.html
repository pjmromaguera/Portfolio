Research & Publications

A Multi-Hazard Interactions Framework for the Philippines

Contributed to the development of a geospatial framework to analyze interactions between multiple hazards, including cascading and compounding effects where one hazard can trigger or amplify another. The framework supports spatial visualization and improved understanding of hazard interdependencies for disaster risk assessment and resilience planning.

AGU Fall Meeting • 2020 Multi-hazard risk Disaster risk analysis

Enhancing Shear Wave Velocity (Vs30) Mapping using HVSR Inversion

Poster presenter at GeoCon 2023 for a study applying HVSR inversion using the MATLAB-based HVSRInv software to estimate shear-wave velocity profiles and Vs30, a critical parameter in seismic hazard and site amplification analysis. An elitist genetic algorithm was used to estimate subsurface layer properties from HVSR data, and results were validated against Refraction Microtremor survey measurements.

GeoCon • 2023 • Poster Presenter Seismic hazard HVSR inversion MATLAB

Conducted remote sensing and geospatial analysis to identify and characterize volcanic features within the Benham Rise, supporting interpretation of submarine volcanic structures and contributing to understanding the geological evolution of the region.
