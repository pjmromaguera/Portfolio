Enhancing Shear Wave Velocity (Vs30) Mapping using HVSR Inversion

Poster presenter at GeoCon 2023 for a study applying HVSR inversion (HVSRInv, MATLAB) to estimate Vs30 for seismic hazard and site amplification analysis. An elitist genetic algorithm was used to estimate subsurface layer properties and results were validated against Refraction Microtremor survey measurements.
